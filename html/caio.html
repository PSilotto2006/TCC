About Caio

Caio Pereira is an experienced therapist specializing in Gestalt Therapy with over 12 years of practice. He operates out of São Paulo, where he is committed to helping clients understand their present experiences and emotions through a holistic approach.

Caio's methodology emphasizes the importance of self-awareness and understanding one's current experience to facilitate personal growth and healing. His empathetic and client-centered approach has made him a respected figure in the field of therapy.
