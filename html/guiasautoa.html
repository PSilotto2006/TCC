Aqui estão alguns dos principais artigos e fontes de autoajuda que podem ser úteis:

  • "The Science of Happiness" - Kashdan, T.B., & Rottenberg, J. (2009) Summary: This article explores the scientific foundations of happiness and how different psychological and behavioral factors can contribute to a more fulfilling life.
  • "Self-Compassion and Psychological Well-Being" - Neff, K.D. (2003) Summary: Kristin Neff examines how self-compassion can promote psychological well-being and improve mental health, offering insights on how to be kinder to oneself.
  • "Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction and Health Benefits: A Meta-Analysis" - Goyal, M., Singh, S., Sibinga, E.M.S., et al. (2014) Summary: This article provides a comprehensive analysis of the benefits of Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR) and how it can help reduce stress and improve overall health.
  • "The Power of Habit: Why We Do What We Do in Life and Business" - Duhigg, C. (2012) Summary: Charles Duhigg explores how habits work and how we can transform them to create positive changes in our personal and professional lives.
  • "Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance" - Duckworth, A.L. (2016) Summary: Angela Duckworth investigates the concept of "grit" and how the combination of passion and perseverance is essential to achieving success.
  • "Emotional Intelligence: Why It Can Matter More Than IQ" - Goleman, D. (1995) Summary: Daniel Goleman explores the importance of emotional intelligence and how it can be more crucial than IQ for personal and professional success.
  • "Atomic Habits: An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits & Break Bad Ones" - Clear, J. (2018) Summary: James Clear offers practical strategies for building positive habits and eliminating negative ones, based on research and real-life examples.
  • "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" - Covey, S.R. (1989) Summary: Stephen R. Covey presents a guide to personal and professional development based on universal principles of effectiveness and leadership.
  • These articles and books offer a variety of approaches and techniques to improve quality of life, from stress and habit management to emotional intelligence and resilience building.
